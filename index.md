Petru M. ("we," "our," or "us") operates the sshremote mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

### Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information from users of the sshremote app.

The app is designed as a client-side tool. All server connection details, such as hostnames, usernames, and passwords or private keys, are stored locally and encrypted on your device only. This information is never transmitted to us or any third party.

### Permissions

The sshremote app requires the following permissions for its core functionality:

*   **Internet:** This permission is used solely to establish SSH connections to the servers that you configure.
*   **Biometric/Authentication:** If enabled, this permission is used to protect access to your stored credentials on your device.

No data is sent to us through the use of these permissions.
